03. Shell Principles

Structural Guidelines

login

Auth Usage

Atmospheric backgrounds only. Focus on whitespace and typography. Avoid heavy enclosing cards that break the "paper" feel.

dock_to_bottom

TopNav Usage

Functional navigation only. Keep headers small and navigation-only. The page title and supporting copy always belong in the content hero section below the navigation shell.

settings

Settings / About

Maintain branding through typography and color palette, but prioritize utility. Standard list patterns are acceptable here.

auto_awesome

Reflection Marker

A subtle functional marker for insights or summaries. Use as a contextual indicator for smart features, never competing with the primary lockup as a sub-brand.
